How to make Icecream at home!

Recipe instructions

Blend the Ingredients Begin by taking a bowl and mixing together your whole milk, rich cream, sugar, and just a tiny pinch of salt. Once these are well combined, stir in your vanilla extract. Feel free to adjust the amount of sugar and vanilla to match your personal taste preferences, ensuring the flavor is just right for you.
Churn the Mixture Next, carefully pour the mixture you've created into a canister. This canister will be used for the churning process. You can churn by using either a hand crank or an electric motor. Continue churning until the ice cream reaches a softly frozen state. This step is important because the agitation helps incorporate air into the mixture, which prevents the formation of large, undesirable ice crystals.
Finally allow it to harden, after churning, transfer your ice cream to a freezer. Allow it to harden further until it reaches the firmness you desire. This final hardening stage ensures that your homemade ice cream has the perfect consistency for serving and enjoying.

Recipe ingredients

- ice cream is good during the summer

Ingredients Amount
Cream ~1 cup
Salt ~1 cup
Vanilla extract, Column 1 ~1 teaspoon
Whole milk ~ 1/2 cup
